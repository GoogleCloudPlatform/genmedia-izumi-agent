## 📖 What is the Co-Director?

The `ads_codirector` is a research-driven video orchestrator that treats the production pipeline as a non-differentiable optimization problem. It implements the **Hierarchical Multi-Agent Architecture**, designed to produce high-fidelity video advertisements under strict narrative and visual constraints.

Unlike static templates, the Co-Director dynamically explores creative strategies, narrative modes, and aesthetic archetypes to find the optimal realization of a user's brief.

## 🔬 The Research Core

The Co-Director implements three foundational research concepts:

1.  **Hierarchical Multi-Agent Architecture**: Decouples global orchestration from specialized pre-production, production, and post-production tasks.
2.  **Global Optimization via MAB**: Uses a **Multi-Armed Bandit** to sample creative configurations. It employs a **Factored Reward Signal** to independently update expected values for Strategy, Mode, and Aesthetics, effectively bypassing the credit assignment problem.
3.  **Local Agentic Self-Refinement**: Implements "feedback descent" to iteratively refine storylines and keyframes. Intermediate artifacts are evaluated by MLLMs and regenerated based on textual feedback before propagating downstream.

## 🏗️ The Multi-Agent Workflow

### 1. Pre-Production ($\Phi_{pre}$)
*   **Creative Brief ($\mathcal{B}$)**: Contextual enrichment of the user prompt into localized cultural resonance.
*   **Storyline ($\mathcal{L}$)**: Scene-by-scene script writing with an integrated LLM self-refinement loop.
*   **Visual Casting ($\mathcal{V}$)**: Character demographic identification and multi-angle wardrobe collage generation.
*   **Storyboard ($\mathcal{S}$)**: Expansion of the script into camera kinematics and temporal constraints.

### 2. Production ($\Phi_{prod}$)
*   **Keyframe Agent ($\Phi_{frame}$)**: Establishes visual priors via first-frame generation to prevent identity drift.
*   **Video Agent ($\Phi_{video}$)**: Transforms keyframes into fluid action using video diffusion models (Veo).
*   **Audio Agent ($\Phi_{audio}$)**: Synthesizes unified narration and background music conditioned on aesthetic archetypes.

### 3. Post-Production ($\Phi_{post}$)
*   **Timeline Stitching**: Consolidates clips and audio into a final rendered MP4, synchronizing visual pacing with acoustic cues.

---

## 🔄 The Optimization Axis (The "Arms")

The Bandit explores a 3D categorical action space to steer the pipeline:

*   **Creative Strategy**: Informational, Transformational, or Comparative.
*   **Narrative Mode**: Analytical, Vignette, or Narrative Drama.
*   **Aesthetic Archetype**: Clarity/Energy, Cinematic Premium, Minimalist Focus, or Kinetic Grit.

## 💡 Izumi Studio Integration

The Co-Director publishes its execution state to two read-only canvases:

1.  **Report Canvas** (Generated by `report_generator.py`):
    *   **Iteration History**: A comprehensive log of every MAB "pull," showing the selected arms and the resulting video.
    *   **Factorized Rewards**: Visual breakdown of strategic efficacy scores (Strategy, Mode, Archetype) vs. global execution quality.
    *   **Agent Feedback**: Detailed self-refinement history and verifier justifications.

2.  **Video Timeline Canvas** (Generated by `stitching_tools.py`):
    *   **Stitching Blueprint**: Visualizes the precise multi-track arrangement of generated video, VO, and BGM clips.
